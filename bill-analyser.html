Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about our Medical Bill Analyzer

How does the bill analyzer detect unnecessary medicines?

+

Our system uses medical guidelines and best practices from reputable medical associations to identify medications that are not typically required for specific conditions. For example, antibiotics are often unnecessarily prescribed for viral infections like the common cold, where they provide no benefit.

We also analyze your prescription in context with the diagnosed condition to flag medications that don't align with standard treatment protocols for that specific illness.

What are commission-based prescriptions?

+

Some pharmaceutical companies offer incentives or commissions to doctors for prescribing their branded medications. Our system identifies potential commission-based prescriptions by analyzing patterns where expensive branded drugs are prescribed when equally effective but much cheaper alternatives (often generic versions) are available.

We compare the prescribed medicines with their generic equivalents and other brands with the same active ingredients to identify significant price differences that may indicate commission-based prescribing.

Is my prescription data kept private?

+

Yes, we take data privacy very seriously. All uploaded prescriptions and manually entered medication data are encrypted and stored securely. We do not share your personal health information with third parties without your explicit consent.

We use anonymized data only for improving our analysis algorithms. You can delete your data at any time from your account settings.

How accurate is the bill analysis?

+

Our system has been trained on millions of prescriptions and medical guidelines, achieving over 90% accuracy in identifying unnecessary and overpriced medications. However, it's important to note that our analysis is meant to be informative, not prescriptive.

Always consult with a healthcare professional before making any changes to your medication regimen. The analysis is designed to empower you with information to have more informed discussions with your doctor.

Can I share the analysis with my doctor?

+

Yes, we encourage transparent conversations about medications with your healthcare providers. You can download or share your analysis report directly with your doctor through the app.

The report is designed to be informative and respectful, presenting alternatives and concerns in a way that facilitates constructive discussion rather than confrontation.
